FAQ
Q: Do I need to be a computer science major to join Colby Hackers?
No! Colby Hackers is for anyone in the Colby community who has an interest in software or computing.
Q: Do we actually "hack" devices?
No. While we do have members that hack secured devices for security research purposes, as a club our focus is on building applications and the software devlopment process.
Q: What is the minimum amount of time I need to commit to Colby Hackers?
Zero minutes. We have members who participate heavily and we have members who participate sparsely. We welcome your participation but do not require it.
Q: How can I get involved?
Good question! Contact Dan Vogel at dhvogel@colby.edu or Brittany Chin at btchin@colby.edu to sign up.
